About us

The problem that we have decided to solve is a way to figure out which movies are the most economically successful based on their box office earnings accumulated from their screening cycles. We want to provide valuable information regarding the popularity and revenue of these movies in any given year. This question is more geared towards the entertainment business and anyone who is interested about movies. The film industry will be able to use this information to get an idea for specific films relating by genre and the movie goers will be able to get their answers regarding some questions they have about the movie. By using the IMDB database we will be able to implement a system that will show the duration, top grossing films, movie ratings by critics, and several other statistics from the past twenty years. Combining all of this into a convenient and interactive web application, will create an enjoyable experience while serving as an aide to answer questions one might have about a movie."
